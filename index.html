Rules & Regulations

  • The CP Contest will run for a period of 2 months, starting from 1st May 2026 to 30th June 2026.
  • A Live Leaderboard Dashboard will be maintained and updated regularly throughout the contest duration to track the performance of participating Channel Partner firms.
  • A minimum of 7 confirmed bookings within the contest period is mandatory for a Channel Partner firm to qualify for the contest reward.
  • Top 4 performing Channel Partner firms (based on total confirmed bookings) will be eligible for the contest reward.
  • In the event of a tie in booking counts, the Channel Partner firm with the higher number of site visits generated during the contest period will be declared the winner.
  • Only confirmed and valid bookings done within the contest duration will be considered eligible.
  • Bookings that are cancelled, refunded, or not realized during the contest period will not be counted towards the final tally.
  • All bookings will be verified and validated internally before finalizing the winners.
  • The reward is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or any other benefit.
  • The travel itinerary, dates, and arrangements for the reward will be decided by the management and communicated to the winners.
  • Any discrepancy, dispute, or malpractice identified during the contest period may lead to disqualification at the sole discretion of the management.
  • The decision of the management will be final and binding in all scenarios related to the contest.
